About Culture

Culture is dedicated to celebrating the vast and vibrant tapestry of music genres that have shaped our world, connected communities, and defined generations. We explore the stories behind the sounds from rebellious punk anthems to complex jazz harmonies highlighting the artists, movements, and moments that changed music history. Whether you’re discovering new genres or deepening your love for familiar ones, Culture is your gateway into the heart of music.

Punk

Sum 41

Sum 41 mixes punk rock energy with metal influences, producing aggressive yet catchy songs.

Genre

Nu‑Metal

Linkin Park

Linkin Park fused rock, metal, and rap, becoming one of the most influential bands of the 2000s.

Korn

Korn pioneered nu‑metal with downtuned guitars, dark themes, and raw emotion.

Limp Bizkit

Limp Bizkit blended metal riffs with hip‑hop swagger and aggressive energy.
